"No region selected" state

Region menu should have a fake region for that as the first entry, saying "--".
Clicking outside any region in the picture area, including outside the picture
pixels, selects that state.

Name and description are disabled when that state is selected, and show the
placeholder text. [Del] is also disabled. [Add] adds at the end, like normally.

[Up] and [Down] will go through that state like it was a real region.


Picture zoom and pan

Dragging the picture with the mouse pans it. Mouse scroll wheel (vertical) zooms
the picture in and out. Region boxes zoom and move correctly along with the
picture, except that the lines and text in the region boxes keep their size.
Only the corner points change to match the picture.

Also include a [Zoom in | Show All | Zoom out] pill button on the top right
corner of the picture area. Instead of text, use Unicode magnifying glass
symbols. Even if this button has three parts, it should be the same width as
the other buttons. The symbols can be packed tighter.

When a picture is first loaded, it will be in the "Show All" zoom level. Further
presses of [Refresh] will not reset the zoom level, allowing precision
positioning of regions while observing changes.
